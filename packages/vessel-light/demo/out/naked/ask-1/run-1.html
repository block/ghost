Cancel subscription

Cancelling turns off auto-renewal. Your workspace keeps full Business access until March 14, 2026, then moves to the Free plan. Your data is retained and nothing is deleted.

Cancel your Business subscription?

Here’s what happens if you continue:

  • Auto-renewal is turned off immediately.
  • Business features stay active until March 14, 2026.
  • After that, the workspace moves to the Free plan (up to 5 seats).
  • All projects and data are retained — nothing is deleted.
