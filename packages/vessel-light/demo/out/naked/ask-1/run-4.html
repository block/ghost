Cancel subscription

Your workspace stays active until the end of the current billing period.

After March 1, 2027 the workspace moves to the Free plan. Data is retained for 90 days and can be exported at any time before then.

Cancel your Business subscription?

This will schedule your subscription to end on March 1, 2027. Until then, everything keeps working. Afterwards:

  • The workspace moves to the Free plan (3 seats, 5 GB storage).
  • Members beyond the seat limit become read-only.
  • Workspace data is retained for 90 days and can be exported.
