Collapse the separate adherence package into the fingerprint and make capabilities opt-in haunts: nodes carry optional `materials` locators, optional haunts attach under `.ghost/haunts/<id>/` (managed by `ghost haunt add|remove|list` or `ghost init --with`), checks become the first haunt at `.ghost/haunts/checks/`, and `ghost review` assembles advisory diff packets from material-backed nodes and checks. The external `ghost haunt <cmd>` dispatch to a second binary is removed.
